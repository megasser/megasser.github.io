Scientists (and tech workers) and militarism

  • Scientists do research for the military; militarism affects the research that is done.
  • Tech workers develop technology for the military.
  • The military recruits scientists on campuses.
  • The military is deeply invested in STEM education.
  • The military is increasingly present in public schools; it is up to scientists to investigate the effects this has.
